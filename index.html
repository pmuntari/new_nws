About Us

Navigation World System Limited is a dynamic private owned company based in West Africa that specializes in Sales, Repair and the Distribution of Integrated Bridge Solutions, Navigation Equipments and other Marine Electronics. With over a decade of experience in Marine Solution we have provided international outreach through our sub-contractor network that meet our customers needs.

Our mission

Our Mission is to conduct efficient SOLAS on Radios and Navigation Equipments, provide High Quality Replacement Parts for vessels around the world and a One-Stop Solution Center for Parts Procurement as well as Bunkering needs.

Our vision

Our vision is working together, combining our strengths, and releasing the potential in each of us. As we act on our five corporate values: customer-centered, think boldly, stewardship, teaming, and collaboration.

What we offer our Clients

Sales and Services

We offer Sales and Services of Integrated Bridge Solutions, Publications and Charts from world renowned manufacturers.
