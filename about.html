Navigation World Systems Limited

We employ highly qualified professionals whose experience is deeply anchored in the shipping industry. Its expertise draws from a proven track record in the distribution of integrated bridge solutions & marine electrical spare parts, including ship automation, hybrid energy, charts & publication, parts repair and bunker brokerage.

Navigation World Systems Limited is partnering with leading operators and technical managers in each segment of its shipping activities to assure best-in-class performance, and innovative solutions aligned with years of specialized experience. On a combined basis, we have 3 office locations spread across West Africa to give you true coverage. Our team enjoys strong relationships with strategic partners across the entire shipping market, including owners, charterers, traders, operators, banks, and brokers.

Mission

Our Mission is to conduct efficient SOLAS on Radios and Navigation Equipments, provide High Quality Replacement Parts for vessels around the world and a One-Stop Solution Center for Parts Procurement as well as Bunkering needs.

Vision

Our vision is working together, combining our strengths, and releasing the potential in each of us. As we act on our five corporate values: customer-centered, think boldly, stewardship, teaming, and collaboration.
