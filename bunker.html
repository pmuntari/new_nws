BUNKER SUPPLIES

Home Bunker Supplies

Millions of dollars are lost every year through disputes over bunker supplies as ships on transit are regularly provided with poor quality marine fuels during bunker stops. which can lead to various engine faults, in the worst-case scenario, can result in a ship losing power. This obviously puts the ship and its crew in danger, with numerous associated consequences. We help preserve risk, by ensuring the quantity delivered from the barge/shore tank and received by the vessel remains the same quantity & quality. As everything we do is in strict adherence to the company Code of Ethics and Compliance policy. All of our supplied marine-fuels meet at least ISO8217:2010 Standards, offering a high quality and globally known compatible product.

With over 9 years of cumulative experience providing bunker supplies, we have gathered an in-depth knowledge on global perspective as well as local insight into real time market dynamics and has earned us stripes amongst our list of enviable customers. Our professional teams in Nigeria, Ghana and Togo are there to manage your enquiries, nominations and deliveries across all time zones, as we are able to arrange third party supplies outside of our usual area using our reliable network of business partners.


We Provide:

  • Automotive Gas Oil (AGO)
  • Premium Motor Spirit (PMS)
  • Lubricating Oil (LUBEOIL)
  • Low & High Pour Oil
  • Grease
  • Fresh Water Supplies
