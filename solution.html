Home Marine Electrical Repairs

Our workshop is recognized for delivering efficient and high-quality repair and maintenance service for integrated bridge solutions, ship automation systems, hybrid energy & power systems, entertainment systems, Navigation and Communication systems, emergency systems, and other marine electrical parts. Our team of expert are committed to delivering exceptional repair services down to component level, using cutting-edge diagnostics technology and tools, offering expert recommendation to customers and performance upgrades. We are constantly working with manufacturer reps to stay abreast of the newest marine electronics products and Training.

As also a distribution network for a wide range of marine spare parts, we provide genuine parts for vessel owners across the West African Coast and nationwide. As we have access to 1000's of parts both new and used, if we don't have a part in stock, we can order it for you and available within few working days. Field technicians are made available to travel throughout West Africa and globally where necessary to conduct any electrical repairs required.

If you have an urgent enquiry, please email or call us on our contact details and we will revert as soon as possible with rates and availability.
